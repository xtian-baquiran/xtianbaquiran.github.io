About Me

Hi, I'm Kristiene Joseph, or just “X” (like the “X” in X-Men or X-mas). I’m a graphic artist with 18 years of experience. My career began at Manila Bulletin, where I built a strong foundation in design by working on broadsheet and tabloid layouts, magazines, and various marketing materials like movie posters, tickets, brochures, and flyers.

Besides layout and graphic design, I also create illustrations—mostly for the lifestyle section and the kids' “Fun Page.” One of my favorite projects was working on the Sports Magazine, where I not only handled layouts and designs but also assisted with photoshoots of featured athletes. Since then, I’ve honed my skills further through roles at other companies, gaining valuable experience along the way.

I also enjoy photography, video editing, painting, and music. I’m an avid gamer and find it a great way to unwind. These hobbies constantly fuel my creativity and provide fresh perspectives for my design work.
